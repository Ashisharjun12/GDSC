Understanding Hunger :
A Global Crisis

Hunger is a global crisis that affects millions of people worldwide. It is a complex issue that is often driven by poverty, lack of access to food, and inadequate food systems. The consequences of hunger go beyond physical health and can impact education, economic development, and social stability...

How Food Waste:
Impact On Poor Communities

While food waste is a global issue that affects everyone, it has a particularly devastating impact on poor communities. With limited access to nutritious food, people living in poverty are more vulnerable to malnutrition and hunger.
